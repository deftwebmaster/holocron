Top 10 Holocrons We’d Kill to Unlock

Galactic historiography has no shortage of enigmatic relics, yet few inspire such obsessive intrigue as the holocron. Compact terabytes of ancient knowledge locked behind Force-sensitive barriers, these artifacts are as desirable as they are dangerous. Their secrets could tilt the balance of power in a galaxy forever teetering on the brink. Here are the top 10 holocrons we’d quite literally kill to unlock — though we might settle for a well-choreographed heist.

1. The Holocron of Heresies#

A spicy morsel from the dark side’s cookbook, this Sith holocron hints at forbidden rituals and long-lost arcana that would give even Palpatine pause. As whispered in quietest corners of Sith academies, they say this holocron contains the emergence of mysteries capable of unraveling the Force itself. Access would require more than a simple picklock — perhaps a barter with fate itself.

2. Jedi Master Arca Jeth’s Warden Holocron#

A relic from the Great Sith War era, Arca Jeth’s holocron serves as an ethical compass for the Jedi. Its guidance has led to the founding of countless philosophies, some of which danced perilously close to the moral edges. While not a reservoir for battle techniques, its true value lies in the judicial wisdom that could bridge or break any council’s consensus.

3. Telos Holocron#

A venerable archive dedicated to Jedi teachings, this holocron was last known to be held by Jedi Master Atris. Infused with the history and ethos of Jedi traditions, it’s a philosophical treasure trove. If one seeks to comprehend the Jedi’s often convoluted moral equations, deciphering Telos would be akin to cracking the code of their sanctimonious enigma.

4. The Revan Holocron#

Revan, the Jedi turned Sith, whose very name elicits a shiver in the spine of force-wielders. The Revan Holocron is said to hold insights into the duality of the Force and strategies from someone who mastered both sides of it. Owning it could yield unparalleled tactical understanding, possibly explaining why so many endeavor to lay claim to this precious artifact.

5. Sith Holocron of Andeddu#

Now, here lies a sinister piece of esoteric machinery. Darth Andeddu’s holocron is the ultimate repository of knowledge on the subject of essence transfer — immortality by another name. Although tearing a soul from its mortal coil tends to endanger one’s continued existence, this holocron’s promise of eternal life is a lure too tempting for many to resist. Handle with peril.

6. The Infinite Empire Holocron#

This Rakatan relic serves as a glimpse into the ancient and harrowing technology of the Infinite Empire. Thought to detail the Star Forge, this holocron potentially holds blueprints to dominate centuries, forcing even the most stalwart Imperial engineer to covet a mere peek. Reverse engineering its contents might trigger a technological renaissance—or catastrophe.

7. Dathka Graush’s Holocron#

Rumor leads us to believe that this artifact contains knowledge used by the dark alchemist himself to craft monstrous servants. A dominion in itself, Dathka’s lore can create super soldiers like Palpatine could only dream of. Of course, if one isn’t careful, it might result in a conga line of hideous failures instead.

8. The Bojhoholocron#

Buried under layers, the Bojhoholocron from the days of the Old Republic tempts with its secrets about Jedi star-maps and hidden enclaves. These coordinates would be catnip to galactic explorers. While most explorers prefer a LEGO Ahsoka E-Wing vs. Shin Hati for such adventures, therein, the boundaries between mystery and fortune dissolve like ice crystals on Tatooine.

9. Exar Kun’s Holocron#

Once in the possession of the rogue Jedi turned dark lord, Exar Kun, this incendiary trinket offers paragons of dark side lore. Kiss fate’s wind and unleash Sith alchemy, battle strategies of Sith aufwiegen, and attain ceremonies lost to memory. But be warned, taming Exar Kun’s spirit will require more than just idle curiosity.

10. Freedon Nadd’s Holocron#

Universally acclaimed as one of the most dangerous Sith holocrons, the Freedon Nadd offers secrets capable of awakening unfathomable power. Nadd’s aura is the definition of madness made sequestered. Its unlocked potential would swing a power trip pivot unlike anything before seen—an empire builder’s dream.

Holding Destiny in Our Hands#

To elucidate, pursuing these holocrons would demand cunning akin to that of Grand Admiral Thrawn, whose rise has been chronicled in timeless offerings like the Original Thrawn Trilogy. Explore Thrawn’s legendary ascendancy here. Navigating complexities he would employ debonair elegance and preempt rivals with an arsenal of uncanny wit.

The price of power persists in making us its knaves — yet for those equipped to pay its toll, the prize is nigh unparalleled.

So here’s to the bold, the brave, and the brash. Whether it requires sleight of hand or deals with informants in outer rim cantinas, persisting on this path promises to dance between perdition and preservation. As ever, the galaxy rewards those who dare.
