🧠 The Definitive Star Wars Trilogy (That Isn’t On Screen)#

“But it was so artfully done…” – Grand Admiral Thrawn

Before the prequels, before Disney — Timothy Zahn’s Thrawn Trilogy was the sequel trilogy fans deserved. These novels defined the Expanded Universe and revived the Star Wars fandom in the early ’90s.

Set five years after Return of the Jedi, the trilogy features a cunning new Imperial mastermind, force-clones, Ysalamiri, and the return of a more world-weary Luke, Leia, and Han.

The trilogy includes:

  • Heir to the Empire

  • Dark Force Rising
    👉 View on Amazon

  • The Last Command
    👉 View on Amazon

Whether you’re deep into Legends or just dipping in — this is where to start.
